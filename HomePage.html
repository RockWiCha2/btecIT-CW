Welcome To Furry Friends

Furry Friends is a therapy dog charity based in Hampshire, dedicated to bringing joy, comfort, and companionship through the calming power of dogs. Our trained therapy dogs visit care homes, schools, hospitals, and community groups, offering support to those who need it most. Whether you’re looking to book a visit, volunteer, or simply learn more about what we do — we’re always excited to connect with fellow dog lovers.

Why Therapy Dogs?

Dogs have an incredible ability to lift our spirits, calm our nerves, and brighten even the darkest days. At Furry Friends, we believe in the unique bond between people and dogs — and we’ve seen first-hand how powerful that connection can be.

Our therapy dogs are gentle, well-trained, and love to meet new people. Whether it’s a friendly tail wag or a quiet cuddle, they offer emotional support that words sometimes can’t.
